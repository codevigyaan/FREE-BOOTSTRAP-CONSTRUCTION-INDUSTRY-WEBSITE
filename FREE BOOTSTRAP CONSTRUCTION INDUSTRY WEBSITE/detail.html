Vastu-Compliant Home Design: A Guide for Modern Indian Families

Vastu Shastra, the ancient Indian science of architecture, continues to influence how homes are designed across the country. From the direction of the main entrance to the placement of the kitchen and puja room, Vastu principles aim to harmonize energy flow and enhance well-being.
